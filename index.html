About Waikato Hospital

Waikato Hospital (founded 1886) serves the greater Waikato area, and is located in Hamilton. As a major regional hospital, Waikato Hospital helps keep NZ's citizens healthy and recover from harm. If you need medical assistance, call 111 or go to the Waikato Hospital ASAP at 183 Pembroke Street, Hamilton. You can also access the Waikato Hospital website by clicking on the name at the top right on any page.

Visiting hours are 11am-1:30pm and 4pm-8pm daily.
