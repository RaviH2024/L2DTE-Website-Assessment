About Waikato Hospital

Waikato Hospital (founded 1886) serves the greater Waikato area, and is located in Hamilton. As a major regional hospital, Waikato Hospital helps keep NZ's citizens healthy and recover from harm. If you need medical assistance, call 111 or go to the Waikato Hospital ASAP at 183 Pembroke Street, Hamilton.
